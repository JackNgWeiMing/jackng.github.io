Introduction

Renggam is a town allocated in south Johor in Malaysia, it take around 82km journey from Johor Bahru. Counting back to a hundred years before it used to be a popular town , you can still imagine the scene that when it is a place it was being developed from the building around.There were cinema, ,kinds of clubs,bus and train station which is a luxury during the past time.

Descent

Since the independence of Malaysia in 1960s,British moved out from Malaysia and many youth people slowly left to seek for a better living.Renggam gradually become a dead town........
